### Appendix A: Risk Assessment Methodology

#### Risk Identification Process
1. Architecture analysis and component identification
2. Security assessment and vulnerability analysis  
3. Business impact and operational risk evaluation
4. Regulatory and compliance requirement mapping

#### Risk Analysis Framework
- **Likelihood Scale:** 1 (Very Low) to 5 (Very High)
- **Impact Scale:** 1 (Very Low) to 5 (Very High)  
- **Risk Rating:** Likelihood × Impact
- **Risk Tolerance:** Critical (20-25), High (12-19), Medium (6-11), Low (3-5), Very Low (1-2)

#### Control Effectiveness Criteria
- **Effective (E):** Properly designed and operating effectively
- **Partially Effective (PE):** Generally adequate with minor gaps
- **Ineffective (I):** Significant design or operational deficiencies
- **Not Implemented (NI):** Required controls not in place

### Appendix B: FSI Risk Categories

1. **Reputational Risk** - Damage to institution's reputation and brand value
2. **Regulatory/Compliance Risk** - Regulatory sanctions, fines, or license loss
3. **Credit Risk** - Financial loss due to counterparty default
4. **Operational Risk** - Loss from inadequate processes, people, systems, or external events
5. **Market Risk** - Losses due to adverse market movements
6. **Liquidity Risk** - Inability to meet financial obligations when due
7. **Technology/Cyber Risk** - Technology failures, cyber attacks, data security breaches
8. **Privacy Risk** - Unauthorized access, use, or disclosure of personal/sensitive data
